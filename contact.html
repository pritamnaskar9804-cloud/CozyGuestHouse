Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times? +
Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.
Do you offer airport transportation? +
Yes! We provide complimentary airport pickup and drop-off service for all our guests. Please inform us of your flight details at least 24 hours in advance.
Is parking available? +
We offer complimentary valet parking for all guests. Your vehicle will be securely stored in our private parking facility.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours will incur a one-night charge. Special event bookings may have different policies.
Are pets allowed? +
Yes, we are pet-friendly! We welcome well-behaved pets with a small additional fee. Please inform us in advance so we can prepare appropriate accommodations.
Do you have a restaurant on-site? +
Yes! Our rooftop restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner with panoramic city views. We also offer 24-hour room service.
